MMU Library Information Resources

General Collection

These are volumes of textbooks and complementary books in the open shelves of the Library. They are available for borrowing over a long period of time.

Reference Collection

The Library keeps a wide range of reference materials that readers consult to obtain authoritative information. They include:-

  • Almanacs
  • Commentaries
  • Atlases
  • Indices
  • Directories
  • Thesaurus

    • Journals and Magazines Collection

    The Library acquires a wide range of print journals and Magazines through subscriptions and donation. These collections give the library users a good base for research.

    Reserve Collection

    These are textbooks and solution manuals that are on high demand and are in few copies the Library. Reserve materials are normally placed on reserve by the lectures in their respective subjects. They are either borrowed for a short time or consulted within the Library.

    Non-Book materials

    The library has a collection of non-print materials which include CD-ROMs and video tapes. These can be borrowed or consulted within the Library

    Internet Resources

    The Library offers internet services to students and staff through ethernet pots in the various reading sections of the Library.
