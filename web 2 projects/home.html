The MultiMedia Library

The mission of the MultiMedia Library is to provide world-class information services that will enable the university to carry out quality teaching, learning and reserach activities. Mmu Library has the capacity to accomodate approximately 1000 students.The Library collection contains materials that support academic reserach as well as materials for personal enrichment. There is also a section of loose periodicalsI(newspapers, magazines and journals) and a MultiMedia(E-resource section) with state-of art computers and internet connectivity to enable wide thorough academic reserach.
